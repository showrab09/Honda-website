Your bike should be serviced every 12 months or 12500 miles; whichever comes sooner if the Honda Service Reminder System (SVRS) has not been activated. The only exception to this is the Civic Diesel, which requires an oil change every 6250 miles or 12 months from the previous oil change.

Tyre pressures should always be checked from cold. Cold means the vehicle has not been moved for at least 3 hours and driven less than 1 mile before checking. Tyre pressure can be checked using a tyre pressure gauge or with equipment at your local garage or petrol station.
